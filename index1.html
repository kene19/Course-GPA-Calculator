How is the GPA calculated?

1. The GPA converter converts each Ethiopia grade equivalent to points using the following scale:

grade points
A+ 4.00
A 4.00
A- 3.75
B+ 3.50
B 3.00
B- 2.75
grade points
C+ 2.50
C 2.00
C- 1.75
D 1.00
F 0.00

2. The points for each class are multiplied by the number of credits or hours for that class, added together, and divided by the total number of credits or hours.

Credits Grade Points

12.0 + 9.0 + 6.0 = 27.0

3 + 3 + 2 = 8

How is the GPA calculated? step by step

1. Add Your Letter Grade

You can select your current letter grade or experiment with a grade estimate to figure out your GPA.

However, for the grades you cannot confirm, you can estimate and choose a grade. Grades can range from A+ to an F on the 4.0 Scale. For each class, choose a letter grade from the drop-down menu.

2. Add Your Class Credits Hour
